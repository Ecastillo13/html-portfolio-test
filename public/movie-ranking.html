Some of the best movies from Eli's perspective:

3 Movies that are on this list:

Coraline

I really like the stop motion animation in this movie as well as in other movies that use that technique, like "Wallace and Gromit", or "The Corpse Bride". It's a really cool way of making interesting visual art while telling a great story.

Napolean Dynamite

This movie has a certain feel to it that is undoubtedly unique. Some don't care for it, but I really like it for it's awkwardness.

Tropic Thunder

This movie is one of the funniest films I've ever seen; stars a lot of bid name actors playing really funny characters. It's really a great piece of art that isn't praised in this current day society.
